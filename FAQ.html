The Path to Our Staff Team

We love when members want to help the community! Being staff means being a positive role model. Here’s what you need to do and know:

Step 1: Join Our Community

Step 2: Attend at a Training Session

If you gonna fail, please don't give up! You can always try again.

Step 3: Passed Training

After passing the Training, you will be promoted to Junior store colleague or if you attended at security training you will be promoted to Junior Guard.

The Path to Our supervisor Team

We love when members want to help the community! Being MR means being a positive role model. Here’s what you need to do and know:

Step 1: Be staff member(SC/SG)

Step 2: Join in our Discord

Step 3: Head down to applications

After you headed down to applications, you will see the application for MR. Fill it out and wait for the results.

Note: Please don't ask for early results, we will announce it when it gonna be checked.
