The Path to Our Staff Team

We love when members want to help the community! Being staff means being a positive role model, professional, and dedicated. Here's your path to joining the team:

Step 1: Join Our Community

  • Join our Roblox group and Discord server
  • Read the rules and familiarize yourself with our community
  • Be active and respectful in-game and on Discord
  • Show maturity and professionalism in all interactions

Step 2: Attend a Training Session

Training sessions are announced in Discord. Don't give up if you don't pass the first time - you can always try again!

  • Check Discord announcements for training times
  • Arrive on time and be prepared
  • Follow instructions carefully
  • Demonstrate professionalism and knowledge
  • Ask questions if you're unsure about anything

Step 3: Pass Training & Get Promoted

After successfully passing training, you'll be promoted to:

  • Junior Kitchen Staff (JKS)

The Path to Management

Becoming a manager is a significant responsibility! MRs help lead the team, train new staff, and ensure smooth operations. Here's how to get there:

Option 1: Apply for Management

Step 1: Be an Active Staff Member

  • You must be a Intern Kitchen leader(IKL) or higher rank
  • Be active in-game and attend shifts regularly
  • Demonstrate professionalism and leadership qualities
  • Help newer staff members and be a positive role model
  • Show dedication to the community

Step 2: Join Our Discord

Make sure you're active in our Discord server - this is where applications are submitted and announcements are made.

Step 3: Submit an Application

When you feel ready:

  • Head to the #staff-announcements channel in Discord
  • Find the Management (MR) application
  • Fill it out completely and honestly
  • Submit and wait for results

Option 2: Get promoted

Step 1: Be an Active Staff Member

  • You must be a Intern manager (IM)
  • Be active in-game and attend shifts regularly
  • Demonstrate professionalism and leadership qualities
  • Help newer staff members and be a positive role model
  • Show dedication to the community

Step 2: Excel in Your Role

  • Consistently perform well during shifts
  • Communicate effectively with other staff and management
  • Show reliability and dedication over time

Step 3: Wait for Promotion Consideration

Promotions are based on merit and availability. Continue to demonstrate your value to the team!

Shift Schedule Information

Shifts are where staff members work together, serve customers, and gain valuable experience! They're hosted by Senior Manager and higher ranks.

Our Shift Schedule

  • Shifts are held regularly throughout the week
  • Announcements are posted in Discord before each shift
  • Shifts typically last 45-60 minutes
  • All staff members are encouraged to attend when available

What Happens During Shifts?

  • Staff work their assigned positions (checkout, food service, etc.)
  • Managers oversee operations and provide guidance
  • Great opportunity to demonstrate your skills for promotions
  • Build camaraderie with other staff members

Benefits of Attending

  • Gain experience and improve your skills
  • Get noticed by management for promotions
  • Earn recognition and rewards
  • Have fun with the community!

Regular attendance at shifts shows dedication and is often considered for promotions. Our supervisors host frequently, but please respect their time and availability!
